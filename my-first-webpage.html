The unicorn is a legendary creature that has been described since antiquity as a beast with a single large, pointed, spiraling horn projecting from its forehead.

Unicorn is also known as monocerus

Unicon Facts

  1. Described as an extremely wild woodland creature.
  2. A symbol of purity and grace
  3. Unicorn could be captured only by a virgin.
  4. Its horn was said to have the power to render poisoned water potable and to heal sickness.
  5. The unicorn continues to hold a place in popular culture.
  6. It is often used as a symbol of fantasy or rarity
